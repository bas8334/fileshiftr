Frequently asked questions

The short version: free, no limits, your files stay yours.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. All conversions happen entirely in your own browser. Your files never leave your computer or phone — we never receive or see them.

Is there a limit on files or conversions?

No, absolutely not. Convert as many files as you want, as often as you want, without an account or waiting. Exactly what frustrated us about other sites.

Does it cost money? Now, or later?

No, it's free and always will be. Fileshiftr runs on contextual ads and voluntary donations from people who found it useful. Find it useful too? Feel free to buy us a coffee — but there's absolutely no pressure.

What file types are supported?

Images: JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC (iPhone photos), GIF and BMP. PDFs: merge, compress, and bundle images into one PDF. More tools coming — have a request? Reach out via the About page.

Does it work on my phone or tablet?

Yes. Fileshiftr works in every modern browser — on iPhone, Android, tablet and computer. Since everything is processed locally, you don't even need a fast internet connection.

Can I convert iPhone photos (HEIC)?

Yes. Just drop your HEIC file into the Convert Image tool — it's automatically detected and converted. The first time, your browser downloads a small helper library (~400 KB); after that it's cached. Your files stay on your device.

Your files stay on your own device. We never store or forward them.
