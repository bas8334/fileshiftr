Privacy Policy

Updated: June 2026 · Effective from the first version

The short version

Your files never leave your browser.
That's not a promise — it's how the technology works.

No server upload No account or registration No tracking cookies No cookie banner needed
1

Your files

We never receive your files. Not temporarily, not encrypted, not in any way.

All conversions — from JPG to PNG, from multiple PDFs to one, from HEIC to WebP — happen entirely in your own browser. Your computer does the work. We are technically not involved.

There is nothing to leak, nothing to hack, nothing to sell. That's not a policy — it's the architecture of the site. Client-side processing is the core of Fileshiftr, not an afterthought.

2

Local browser storage

Fileshiftr stores two small pieces of information via your browser's localStorage. This stays exclusively on your device — it is never sent to a server and we cannot see it.

Key What it stores Purpose
conv_total Number, e.g. "12" Counts how many times you've converted a file. After a number of conversions we ask once, politely, for a donation.
nudge_done "1" or empty Remembers if you've already seen a donation prompt, so we don't repeat it.

This data contains no personal information and never leaves your device. You can clear it via Browser settings → Advanced → Clear site data. The counter then resets to zero.

3

Hosting and server logs

Fileshiftr is hosted via Cloudflare Pages. When you visit the site, the hosting provider stores standard access logs: your IP address, the page visited and the time. This is standard for every website on the internet and technically unavoidable.

We do not actively consult these logs for analysis or advertising purposes. Cloudflare has its own privacy policy and retention periods.

4

External services

To function properly, Fileshiftr loads a number of files from external servers. With each of these requests your browser sends your IP address — this is technically unavoidable. We are transparent about this.

Google Fonts — Our fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google receives your IP address in the process. This is a known GDPR grey area; we are considering self-hosting the fonts in the future. Until then: if you want to prevent this, you can block Google Fonts via a browser extension — the page will work fine with system fonts.
jsDelivr CDN — For HEIC conversion the page loads the heic2any library from jsDelivr. Your IP address is sent to their servers. The library is then cached by your browser; the request only happens once per device.
cdnjs (Cloudflare) — For PDF processing the page loads pdf-lib from Cloudflare's public CDN. Same principle as above: once, then cached.
None of these services receive your files — only program code is loaded, not your photos or documents.
5

Advertisements

Fileshiftr currently has no advertisements. If we add them, we will do so via EthicalAds or Carbon Ads — services that show contextual ads based on page content, not based on your browsing profile.

Geen profiling. No tracking cookies. No cookie banner needed — ook niet bij advertenties.

This is a deliberate choice: it fits with Fileshiftr's privacy promise. Once ads are active, we will update this policy.

6

Your rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the right to access, correct, delete and port your personal data, and the right to object to processing.

In practice, Fileshiftr processes virtually no personal data. The only data technically processed are IP addresses in standard server logs from our hosting provider. For questions about this, or to submit a GDPR request, you can contact us via the details below.

7

Contact

Questions or comments about this privacy policy? Send an email to privacy@fileshiftr.com. We respond as quickly as possible, within thirty days at the latest.

For general questions or feedback about the tools you can also visit the About page.
