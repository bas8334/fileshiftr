Cut out the background without uploading your photo

Removing a background used to mean a fiddly selection tool, or handing your photo to a website that uploads it to their servers. This does neither. An AI model runs directly inside your browser, looks at your photo, and separates the subject from everything behind it — then hands you a clean cut-out. Your image never leaves your device, so there's nothing to worry about and no one looking at your photos.

It's free with no catch: no watermark stamped across the result, no account, no daily cap. Cut out one product photo or two hundred portraits — the only wait is the one-time model download on your first visit.

The honest trade-off

Because the AI runs on your own device, the first photo takes longer while the model downloads once. In return, your photo is never uploaded, there's no limit, and every photo after the first is quick. It's the same privacy a desktop app gives you — without installing anything.

What makes a clean cut-out

The model is good at tricky edges — hair, fur, the gap between an arm and a body — but the photo still matters. A subject that stands out clearly from its background gives the cleanest result. Very low contrast (a grey cat on a grey couch) or busy, cluttered backgrounds are harder, just as they would be for a person with a selection tool. If an edge isn't perfect, a slightly clearer photo usually fixes it.

Why the first run takes a moment

To keep your photo on your device, the AI model has to run on your device too — which means your browser downloads it once (a few hundred MB) the first time you use the tool. That download is the only real wait. Your browser caches it, so the next photo, and your next visit, load it instantly. It's the price of not uploading your images anywhere, and we think it's a fair one.

Frequently asked questions

Free, unlimited, and your photos stay on your device.

Are my photos uploaded to remove the background?

No. The AI model that removes the background runs entirely in your own browser, on your own device. Your photos are never sent to a server — not even to us. That's the core difference with sites like remove.bg.

Why does the first photo take longer?

The first time you use the tool, your browser downloads the AI model once (a few hundred MB) and caches it. That download is the only wait — after it finishes, every photo is processed in a few seconds, and on your next visit the model loads from cache instantly.

Is there a limit on how many images I can process?

No. Remove the background from as many photos as you want, as often as you want — no daily cap, no account, no waiting between files.

What do I get back — a transparent image?

By default you get a PNG with a transparent background (the alpha channel), ready to drop onto any design. You can also choose a solid white or custom-colour background instead before you download.

How good is the quality on hair and fine edges?

This tool uses a high-quality matting model, so it handles tricky edges like hair, fur and semi-transparent areas far better than a simple cut-out. Results depend on the photo: a clear subject against a contrasting background gives the cleanest edges.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes, on modern phones. Because the AI runs on your device, an older or low-memory phone may be slow or run out of memory on very large photos. On a computer with a recent browser it's fastest, especially where hardware acceleration (WebGPU) is available.

Is it really free, with no watermark or sign-up?

Yes. No watermark on your images, no account, no email, no trial that expires. It's free and stays that way.
